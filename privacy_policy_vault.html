Privacy Policy for Vault

Effective Date: May 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Vault - Photo Organizer macOS application ("Vault", "we", "us", or "our").

1. Data Collection and Storage

Vault is built with a strict privacy-first philosophy. We do not collect, store, process, transmit, or share any personal data, images, or usage metrics.

All of your photos, albums, and application data are stored entirely locally on your Mac. We have absolutely no access to your files, your library, or any information regarding how you use the App.

2. Third-Party Services & Tracking

Vault operates completely independently. It does not include any third-party SDKs, analytics trackers, telemetry services, or external monetization frameworks (such as RevenueCat, Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.). Your activity is never tracked.

3. Network Access

The App does not require an active internet connection to function. Your data never leaves your device.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect your email address or any contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
