Privacy Policy

DiffDrop

DiffDrop is a macOS utility that compares files locally on your device. It does not collect, transmit, sell, or track your data.

Last Updated

May 13, 2026

Information We Collect

DiffDrop does not collect personal information. The app does not create user accounts, does not use analytics, does not show ads, and does not include tracking technologies.

Files You Compare

When you choose or drop files into DiffDrop, those files are processed locally on your Mac. File contents, file names, metadata, previews, hashes, and comparison results are not sent to the developer or to any server operated by the developer.

DiffDrop uses the selected files only to perform the comparison you requested. The app does not upload your files and does not maintain a cloud copy of them.

Data Storage

DiffDrop does not store a server-side history of your comparisons. Comparison information is handled on-device. You can clear the current comparison inside the app.

Tracking, Analytics, and Advertising

  • No third-party advertising SDKs are included.
  • No third-party analytics SDKs are included.
  • No data is used to track you across apps or websites.

Permissions

DiffDrop accesses files only when you choose them through the file picker or drop them into the app. The app does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, photos, or calendar.

Third-Party Services

DiffDrop does not send your files or personal information to third-party services. Apple may process information related to App Store downloads, purchases, crash reports, or diagnostics according to Apple's own privacy practices.

Children's Privacy

DiffDrop is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if DiffDrop's privacy practices change. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: diffdrop@matisandoval.com
