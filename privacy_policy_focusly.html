Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 30, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Focus - Pomodoro & Tasks ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our iOS application.

1. Information We Collect

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our app collects minimal data necessary to provide our services.

1.1 Data Collected

We collect the following types of data through our third-party service provider (RevenueCat):

  • User ID: An anonymous identifier generated automatically to manage your app installation and purchases
  • Device ID: A device-level identifier used for fraud prevention and purchase validation
  • Purchase History: Records of your in-app purchases to enable premium features and restore purchases

Important: All data collected is anonymous and not linked to your personal identity. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used exclusively for:

2.1 App Functionality

  • Unlocking premium features (themes, unlimited tasks, advanced statistics)
  • Restoring purchases when you reinstall the app
  • Validating purchase transactions
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring security
  • Providing customer support

2.2 Analytics

  • Understanding app performance and revenue metrics
  • Measuring conversion rates and user retention
  • Improving app functionality and user experience

3. Data Storage and Processing

Your data is processed and stored by RevenueCat, our third-party payment and subscription management service. RevenueCat maintains industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

For more information about RevenueCat's privacy practices, please visit: RevenueCat Privacy Policy

4. What We Don't Do

We want to be clear about what we do not do with your data:

  • ❌ No Tracking: We do not track you across other apps or websites
  • ❌ No Advertising: We do not display ads or share your data with advertising networks
  • ❌ No Marketing: We do not send marketing emails or promotional communications
  • ❌ No Data Selling: We never sell your data to third parties or data brokers
  • ❌ No Personal Information: We do not require or collect personal information like email or name
  • ❌ No Location Tracking: We do not collect or access your location data

5. User Data on Device

The following data is stored locally on your device and never leaves your device:

  • Your tasks and task completion history
  • Pomodoro session statistics (daily, weekly, monthly)
  • Timer duration preferences
  • Selected theme preferences
  • Total focus time

This data is stored using iOS UserDefaults and remains completely private to you.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not target children.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Data Deletion: You can delete all local data by uninstalling the app
  • Purchase History: Contact us to request deletion of your purchase history
  • No Account Required: You can use the app without creating an account or providing personal information

8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:

  • RevenueCat: For payment processing and subscription management. View their privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data.
