1. Privacy Policy

1.1 Overview

We respect your privacy. Flashy does not require you to create an account and does not collect personally identifiable information. All payments are processed securely through Apple's In-App Purchase system.

1.2 Data We Do Not Collect

  • No personal names, emails, or contact information
  • No GPS or precise location data
  • No tracking for advertising purposes
  • No third-party analytics

1.3 Data We Process

To operate properly, we process limited technical and transactional data:

  • Apple receipts – to verify subscriptions
  • Prompts & AI responses – temporarily processed via OpenAI API to generate flashcards
  • Usage counts – number of flashcards generated, stored in Firebase

1.4 How We Use Data

  • To validate subscriptions and restore purchases
  • To deliver AI-generated flashcards
  • To ensure service stability and prevent abuse

1.5 Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary: Apple receipts are kept per tax requirements; prompts are transient and not stored long term. OpenAI may retain data for up to 30 days for abuse monitoring, per its privacy policy.

1.6 Security

All connections are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. The OpenAI API key is securely stored on our Firebase backend and never exposed to users.

1.7 Third-Party Providers

Apple — Payments & subscriptions

Firebase (Google) — Backend hosting

OpenAI — AI text generation

RevenueCat — Subscription management

1.8 Children's Privacy

Flashy is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from minors.
