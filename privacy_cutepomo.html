Cute Pomo

Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 17, 2025

Overview

Cute Pomo is a simple Pomodoro timer application for macOS that helps you manage your time and stay focused.

Data Collection

Cute Pomo does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All settings and preferences are stored locally on your device using macOS UserDefaults.

Information We Don't Collect

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not track your usage
  • We do not use analytics services
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not require an account or login

Local Storage

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Timer preferences (work duration, break duration)
  • Visual preferences (colors, fonts, window size)
  • Notification settings
  • Custom timer presets

This data never leaves your device and is only used to maintain your app preferences.

Third-Party Services

Cute Pomo does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date.
